OUR MISSION

At The Happy HeART studio we are not simply concerned with making beautiful art, we are also concerned with developing a beautiful heart.

It is our objective to foster student creativity, build self-esteem and resilience, teach children how to self-love and self care, and positively impact others to create a peaceful, compassionate, and loving world.
